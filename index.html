At 65, Gulshan Nagpal is not just a retired banker or an established industrialist — he is a passionate motivational speaker and life coach, devoting his energy to shaping a better society. With a background rooted in financial hardships, and later decades of service in the banking sector, he chose to embark on this new chapter not for monetary gain, but purely out of passion.

His sessions focus on youth empowerment, good parenting, women upliftment, and self-development for working professionals. Despite building a successful business — Rainbow Infra Solutions Pvt. Ltd. — post-retirement, his vision today is to guide the next generation toward a better, more meaningful life.

Vision

To create a generation of emotionally strong, socially aware, and self-driven individuals who contribute meaningfully to society.

Mission

To inspire and empower youth, parents, and professionals through real-life experiences, workshops, and motivational sessions that build clarity, purpose, and character.

Aim

Mr. Gulshan Nagpal’s aim is to build a progressive and emotionally intelligent society, starting with its foundation—youth, families, and professionals. He believes that real transformation begins when individuals are guided with values, purpose, and self-awareness.
